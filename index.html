Meeting Place and Time

We meet at the Griffis Institute at 725 Daedalian Dr, Rome NY. We meet every other Tuesday from 2:30 to 5:00 pm year round. Though if school is canceled due to inclement weather or a holiday, then we also cancel meeting for that day.

We generally meet in the Board Room, but if that room is occupied, we may be in the conference room or the auditorium.
